Our Mission & Vision

Unveiling the hidden treasures of Western Odisha and empowering its local communities through sustainable tourism.

Our Story

Heritage Yatri was born from a deep love for Western Odisha—a land of rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes. We saw a region full of undiscovered treasures, from ancient temples and serene waterfalls to unique local cuisines.

However, we also saw that the travel experience was often fragmented, leaving tourists to navigate a confusing web of different apps and unreliable information for bookings, transport, and local guidance.

We believed there had to be a better way. Our mission is to create a single, seamless platform that connects travelers with authentic local experiences, making trip planning effortless while promoting sustainable tourism that directly benefits the communities of Western Odisha.

How We Create Impact

For the Traveler

We provide a reliable and convenient platform to handle every aspect of your journey, ensuring a safe and memorable experience.

For the Community

We are committed to empowering the local economy by bringing regional hotels, restaurants, and artisans into the digital world.

For Odisha's Heritage

We drive awareness and appreciation for cultural sites, contributing to the preservation of Western Odisha’s unique identity.
